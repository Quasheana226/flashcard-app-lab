How to Use

📚 Learn

Choose a category and start learning BMW parts. Each flashcard presents a question on the front and the answer on the back.

🔄 Flip

Click the flashcard or press SPACE to flip between the question and answer. Test your knowledge before revealing the solution.

🎯 Navigate

Use the Previous and Next buttons or arrow keys (← →) to move through the flashcards. Track your progress in the stats bar.

🌓 Theme

Toggle between light and dark themes using the moon icon in the header. Your preference is saved automatically.
